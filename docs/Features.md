# Features

## Summary

- Provides many different Warnings when the player is missing important items from their inventory, such as appropriately leveled Health Potions, reagents, etc.
- Shows what Abilities are available to train, or will be available at the next training level.

## Mini Viewer

- Shows information from the Summary, but in a nice mini panel.

## Class Abilities

- Comprehensive database of all class abilities, along with their training cost, source, and recommendation as to training / HC usefulness.
